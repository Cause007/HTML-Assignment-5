Our Story

The Covid Pandemic had an effect on all of us.

Through 2020, I drank beer... AND had a kid. I was getting older and had less time available for showering, getting dressed, and all that "young" stuff. So with financial help from MY dad, we opened a gym where we didn't have to be clean or smell good, because as a dad, who has time for that?

Our Motto

Your body is yours. Your scent is yours. This gym is ours. Stay healthy.
